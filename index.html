About Us

We are a company that produces high-quality organic coffees and unique flavors. The Vila das Oliveiras coffee was born through the passion for the daily ritual of making artisan coffee at home. Such a simple habit, but one that brings us comfort and familiarity. We produce coffees that generate memories through aroma and flavor. We are able to transport it to another place through taste.

We have a physical space in the city of Alfredo Wagner, SC. We receive people from different ethnicities, places, and cultures. Oh, the stories that pass through our establishment inspire us to create new products that have a storytelling related to the people we know. After all, this all happened thanks to everyone who supported us.
